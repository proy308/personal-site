The Strategic Pivot Lab

A specialized mentoring initiative for professionals pivoting to Strategy, VC, and Investment—or seeking clarity in their broader career narrative.

The Problem

"Most mentorship is too polite. High-performers don't need cheerleaders; they need the Commercial Translation Layer that turns technical expertise into strategic authority."

The Philosophy: Bias for Action

The "Lawyer's Trap"

Most high-performers suffer from Analysis Paralysis. You treat every career move like a "One-Way Door" (irreversible risk), leading to 5-year plans that never happen.

Result: You stay safe, but you stay stuck.

The Lab Approach

You can't think your way into a new career; you have to act your way into it. We treat pivots as "Two-Way Doors" (reversible experiments).

Result: We design low-risk "runway experiments" to test your fit for VC/Strategy before you quit your job.

The Framework

  • Identity Redlining: A brutal audit to strip away the "support function" tag and build a strategic leader identity.
  • Commercial Fluency: Moving beyond "legal review" to "Risk Architecture"—learning to read P&Ls and 10-Ks like a deal-maker.
  • Strategic Experimentation: Replacing 5-year plans with 6-month "runway experiments" to validate your pivot without blowing up your career.
