The Strategic Pivot Lab

A specialized mentoring initiative for professionals pivoting to Strategy, VC, and Investment—or seeking clarity in their broader career narrative.

The Mission

We provide general career guidance and specialized "brand audits" for high-performers. The Lab is designed to help you translate your existing skills into a language that business leaders and investors value.

  • Narrative Architecture: Moving from a "technical" tag to a "strategic leadership" identity.
  • Commercial Intelligence: Learning to view your career moves through a commercial and ROI lens.
  • Career Audits: 1-on-1 sessions to identify "spiky" traits that recruiters in Strategy and VC look for.
